Daisy

An old man, a dream, and a journey of salvation

BackGround Story

One day, an officer from the government visited the old man and told him that his house was too old and dangerous to live inside. The officer wanted him to move to the nursing house.

The old man told the officer that he had lived in this house for many years with his wife Daisy, who was on the way back from her travel in another country. He would like to wait until his wife came back and then decide what to do.

In the night of that day, the old man fell into a strange dream. In that dream, there is a voice calling him and leading him to fly through the whole city…

How to play

Click screen to fly up, flick to control the direction

Avoid obstacles: buildings, coming clouds. Substracting points if the old man runs into them

There are several levels. Every one of them corresponds to a time period of the old man’s life with Daisy. Passing the level could unlock the memory of that time period.
